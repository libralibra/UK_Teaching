What is a Personal Tutor for?

The personal tutoring system in higher education sector is to:

  • Encourage you to be a confident learner
  • Guide you to participate in the academic community actively
  • Support you in dealing with possible challenges that may affect your university life
  • Prompt your to reflect your personal progress and development
  • Draw your attention to services and possible opportunities that may benefit you if possible

What does a Personal Tutor Do?

Typically, a personal tutor will:

  • Listen to your non-academic experience of university life
  • Give your advice on pastoral matters
  • Offer you insights into general study skills (non-subject-specific)
  • Guide you to specialised services provided by the university (if necessary)
  • Write a reference letter when you apply for a job

Meeting Format
